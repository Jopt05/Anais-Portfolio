About

me

My name is Anais Hernández Rivera and i'm a digital animation and visual effects engineer from the Tec of Coatzacoalcos. Through my career i've made a lot of projects both with illustrations and software like Illustrator, Maya, Blender and Animate.

There is a lot of things that make me happy but drawing (and my pets) is the perfect hobby for me. I use a lot of techniques for drawing such as hatching, star light and blending. I like to express myself trough drawing and crafting and I use both of them to show my love and apprecition to people close to me. I put every feeling and thought in my art with the expectation and effort of doing a great job.

I hope that you like my art and if you wanna collaborate with me in any moment, feel free to make contact with me. I'll make my biggest effort to reach that project that you have in mind.

Thank you.
